About TEDx Gwagalada

TEDx Gwagalada is an independently organized TED event bringing together thought leaders, innovators, and changemakers to share ideas that matter.

Our Mission

We believe in the power of ideas to change the world. TEDx Gwagalada brings together diverse voices from our community and beyond to share breakthrough ideas, spark conversations, and inspire action.

Our event focuses on innovation, technology, education, and community development, highlighting solutions that can transform lives and create lasting impact.
